# DGCP Governance: Governance Reporting Standards

**Effective Date:** 26 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Periodic Governance Reports and Statements

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines standardized reporting requirements for DGCPâ„¢ governance activities.

---

## 2. Report Types

Governance reports may include:
- Annual governance summaries
- Incident and enforcement reports
- Audit outcome statements
- Governance roadmap updates

---

## 3. Reporting Integrity

Reports must be:
- Evidence-referenced
- Timestamped
- Consistent with governance index and changelog

---

## 4. Public Accountability

Reports serve as accountability instruments and may be cited by auditors, institutions, and stakeholders.
