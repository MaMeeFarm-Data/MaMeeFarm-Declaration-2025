# DGCP Governance: Financial System Interface

**Effective Date:** 22 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Banks, Financial Institutions, and Auditors

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how DGCP™ data interfaces with formal financial systems.

---

## 2. Financial Recognition Principle

DGCP™ data may be used to:
- Support credit assessment
- Validate income-generating activity
- Demonstrate operational continuity

DGCP™ does not act as a financial intermediary.

---

## 3. Non-Substitution Clause

DGCP™ data:
- Supplements financial evaluation
- Does not replace regulatory compliance
- Does not guarantee credit approval

---

## 4. Liability Boundary

Financial institutions bear responsibility for final decisions.
