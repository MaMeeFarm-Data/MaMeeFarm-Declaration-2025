# DGCP Governance: Public Sector Adoption Readiness

**Effective Date:** 23 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Governmental and Public Institutions

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines governance readiness for adoption of DGCP™ by public sector entities.

---

## 2. Public Sector Compatibility

DGCP™ supports:
- Auditability
- Transparency
- Non-repudiation
- Data minimization

Without requiring proprietary infrastructure.

---

## 3. Adoption Boundary

Public sector adoption:
- Does not transfer ownership
- Does not override DGCP™ governance
- Requires explicit agreements

---

## 4. Accountability

Public institutions remain accountable for regulatory compliance and decision-making.
