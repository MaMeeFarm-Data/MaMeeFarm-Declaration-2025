# DGCP Governance: Data Retention & Deletion

**Effective Date:** 29 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Data Retention and Removal Decisions

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs retention duration and deletion handling of DGCP™ data.

---

## 2. Retention Principle

DGCP™ prioritizes:
- Long-term verifiability
- Audit continuity
- Historical integrity

Retention periods are defined by governance, not convenience.

---

## 3. Deletion Boundary

Deletion may apply to:
- Private raw data
- Access-controlled materials

Deletion does **not** apply to:
- Public proofs
- Hashes
- Timestamps
- Governance records

---

## 4. Governance Control

All deletion actions require:
- Documented justification
- Governance approval
- Public reference where appropriate
