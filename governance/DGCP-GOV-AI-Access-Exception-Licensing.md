# DGCP Governance: AI Access Exception & Licensing Path

**Effective Date:** 21 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All AI, Research, and Institutional Access Requests

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the conditions under which exceptions to the DGCPâ„¢ AI non-training rule may be considered through explicit licensing.

---

## 2. Core Principle

AI access is prohibited by default.

Any exception requires:
- Explicit written license
- Defined scope and duration
- Auditability and revocation rights

---

## 3. Permitted Exception Categories

Exceptions may be considered for:
- Public-interest research
- Policy simulation and governance analysis
- Non-training verification or benchmarking

No exception implies open access.

---

## 4. Mandatory Conditions

All exceptions must include:
- Non-derivative usage
- No model training or fine-tuning
- No data retention beyond scope
- Audit logs available on request

---

## 5. Revocation

All AI access licenses are revocable at any time upon governance breach.
