# DGCP Governance: System Deprecation & Fork Prevention

**Effective Date:** 21 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Protocol Implementations

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs system deprecation, protocol evolution, and prevention of unauthorized forks.

---

## 2. Deprecation Rules

Deprecated components:
- Must remain auditable
- Cannot be erased
- Must be clearly labeled

---

## 3. Fork Prevention

Unauthorized forks of DGCP™:
- Are not recognized
- Cannot claim governance legitimacy
- Cannot inherit trust or audit lineage

---

## 4. Governance Continuity

Only MaMeeFarm™ governance may authorize:
- Protocol evolution
- Fork recognition
- Trust inheritance
