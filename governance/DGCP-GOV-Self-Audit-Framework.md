# DGCP Governance: Self-Audit Framework

**Effective Date:** 26 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Internal Governance Reviews

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes a formal self-audit framework for DGCP™ governance and operations.

---

## 2. Audit Scope

Self-audits assess:
- Governance policy compliance
- Proof handling and integrity
- License enforcement consistency
- Documentation accuracy

---

## 3. Audit Independence

Self-audit results must be:
- Documented
- Timestamped
- Publicly referenceable
- Open to external verification

---

## 4. Corrective Actions

Findings require:
- Documented remediation
- Governance review approval
- Public audit trail updates
