# DGCP Governance: Governance Metrics & KPIs

**Effective Date:** 26 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Governance Operations and Reviews

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure effectiveness, integrity, and resilience of DGCP™ governance.

---

## 2. Core Governance Metrics

DGCP™ governance metrics include:
- Proof integrity rate (validated vs. invalidated)
- Audit completeness ratio
- Governance response time
- Enforcement resolution consistency
- Documentation currency index

---

## 3. KPI Principles

KPIs must be:
- Verifiable
- Non-gameable
- Governance-relevant
- Publicly auditable where appropriate

---

## 4. Review Cycle

Governance metrics are reviewed periodically and recorded via public governance commits.
