# DGCP Governance: Appeals & Dispute Resolution

**Effective Date:** 24 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Governance Disputes and Enforcement Appeals

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines a formal process for appeals and dispute resolution within DGCPâ„¢ governance.

---

## 2. Right to Appeal

Affected parties may appeal:
- Enforcement actions
- Proof invalidation decisions
- License revocations

Appeals do not suspend cryptographic records.

---

## 3. Review Process

Appeals are reviewed based on:
- Evidence integrity
- Governance compliance
- Procedural fairness

---

## 4. Finality

Appeal outcomes are documented, timestamped, and publicly auditable where appropriate.
