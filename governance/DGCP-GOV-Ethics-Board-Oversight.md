# DGCP Governance: Ethics Board & Oversight

**Effective Date:** 24 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Governance, Proof, and Enforcement Decisions

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes an ethical oversight mechanism within the DGCP™ framework to ensure governance decisions remain aligned with human dignity, fairness, and proportionality.

---

## 2. Ethics Board Role

The Ethics Board may:
- Review sensitive governance decisions
- Advise on ethical conflicts
- Recommend corrective actions

The board does not override cryptographic truth or evidence layers.

---

## 3. Oversight Scope

Ethical oversight applies to:
- Enforcement actions affecting contributors
- Public disclosures
- High-impact licensing decisions

---

## 4. Non-Interference Principle

Ethical review may guide governance but may not alter:
- Historical proofs
- Cryptographic timestamps
- Audit records
