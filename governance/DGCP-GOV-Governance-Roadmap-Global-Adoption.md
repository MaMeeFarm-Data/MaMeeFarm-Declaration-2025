# DGCP Governance: Roadmap to Global Adoption

**Effective Date:** 25 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Long-Term DGCP™ Governance Strategy

---

## 1. Purpose

This document outlines a governance roadmap guiding DGCP™ from pilot-scale implementation to global adoption.

---

## 2. Roadmap Phases

- Phase I: Proof integrity and local audit trust
- Phase II: Multi-entity governance expansion
- Phase III: Public sector and institutional onboarding
- Phase IV: International and treaty-level alignment

---

## 3. Governance Control

Roadmap progression requires:
- Public governance review
- Changelog updates
- Explicit approval at each phase
