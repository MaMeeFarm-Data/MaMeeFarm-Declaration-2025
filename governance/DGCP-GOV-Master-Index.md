# DGCP Governance: Master Index

**Effective Date:** 30 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Master Reference  
**Scope:** Entire MaMeeFarm™ DGCP™ Governance Framework

---

## Purpose

This document serves as the master index for all DGCP™ governance policies.

It provides a single authoritative navigation point for:
- Auditors
- Regulators
- Institutional partners
- Internal governance operations

---

## Governance Domains Covered

DGCP™ governance includes, but is not limited to:

- Platform Non-Reliance
- Time Authority Hierarchy
- Proof Invalidation & Revocation
- Data Ownership & Custody
- AI Non-Training & Access Exceptions
- Economic Rights & Monetization Boundaries
- Risk & Threat Models
- Ethics, Appeals, and Oversight
- Metrics, Audits, Transparency, Reporting
- Privacy, Safety-by-Design, Anonymization
- Data Lifecycle, Retention, Federation
- Partnerships, Maturity Levels, Succession
- Emergency, Crisis, and Continuity Protocols

Each policy maintains its own effective date and scope.

---

## Authority Statement

This master index does not override individual governance policies.  
It exists to consolidate discovery and reference.
