# DGCP Governance: Governance Maturity Levels

**Effective Date:** 25 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Implementations and Deployments

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance maturity levels for DGCP™ to support phased adoption, evaluation, and scaling.

---

## 2. Maturity Levels

### Level 1 — Local Verified Operation
- Single-entity governance
- Local proof generation
- Public audit ledger active

### Level 2 — Multi-Entity Governance
- Multiple contributors
- Shared governance rules
- Registry-based coordination

### Level 3 — Institutional Readiness
- Formal governance bodies
- Legal and audit integration
- Cross-jurisdiction compatibility

### Level 4 — Global Governance Network
- International alignment
- Treaty readiness
- Distributed governance oversight

---

## 3. Evaluation

Maturity progression is determined by governance review, not self-declaration.
