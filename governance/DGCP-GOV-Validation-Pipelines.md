# DGCP Governance: Validation Pipelines

**Effective Date:** 27 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Validation and Verification Workflows

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines standardized validation pipelines for verifying DGCP™ data and proofs.

---

## 2. Validation Stages

DGCP™ validation pipelines include:
- Format and schema validation
- Hash and integrity verification
- Timestamp verification (OTS / ledger)
- License and governance compliance checks

---

## 3. Automation & Oversight

Validation may be automated but must remain:
- Transparent
- Auditable
- Subject to human oversight

---

## 4. Validation Outcomes

Validation results are recorded as:
- Verified
- Verified with annotations
- Invalidated (per governance rules)
