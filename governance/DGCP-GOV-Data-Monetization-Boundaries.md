# DGCP Governance: Data Monetization Boundaries

**Effective Date:** 22 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Revenue-Generating Use of DGCP™ Data

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines clear boundaries for monetizing DGCP™ data while preserving ethical and governance integrity.

---

## 2. Allowed Monetization

Allowed monetization pathways:
- Time-bound data licenses
- Purpose-specific access
- Verification-as-a-service
- Audit support and compliance tooling

---

## 3. Forbidden Monetization

Forbidden practices include:
- Bulk data resale
- Unrestricted sublicensing
- AI training monetization
- Data broker redistribution models

---

## 4. Transparency Requirement

All monetized uses must be:
- Contractually explicit
- Auditable
- Revocable upon breach
