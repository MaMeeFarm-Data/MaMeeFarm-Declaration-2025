# DGCP Governance: Adversarial Resistance

**Effective Date:** 23 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Adversarial Scenarios Affecting DGCP™

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance responses to adversarial actions against DGCP™.

---

## 2. Adversarial Actors

Potential adversaries include:
- Unauthorized data aggregators
- AI model developers scraping data
- Platform operators asserting control
- Malicious forks or protocol mimics

---

## 3. Resistance Mechanisms

DGCP™ resists adversarial action through:
- Cryptographic anchoring
- License-based enforcement
- Public audit trails
- Non-reliance on centralized platforms

---

## 4. Non-Escalation Principle

DGCP™ governance prioritizes:
- Evidence-based response
- Public clarification
- Legal proportionality

Escalation occurs only when necessary.
