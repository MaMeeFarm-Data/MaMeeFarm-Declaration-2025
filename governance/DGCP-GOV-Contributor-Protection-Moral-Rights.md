# DGCP Governance: Contributor Protection & Moral Rights

**Effective Date:** 21 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Human Contributors to DGCP™ Data

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy protects the moral and attribution rights of all contributors whose real work generates DGCP™ data.

---

## 2. Moral Rights Principle

Contributors retain:
- Attribution rights
- Context integrity rights
- Protection against misrepresentation

---

## 3. Prohibited Actions

It is prohibited to:
- Remove contributor attribution
- Recontextualize proofs misleadingly
- Use data to harm contributors

---

## 4. Enforcement

Violations trigger:
- Proof invalidation
- License revocation
- Public clarification where necessary
