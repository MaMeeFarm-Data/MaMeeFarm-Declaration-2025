# DGCP Governance: Treaty Readiness Declaration

**Effective Date:** 22 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Declaration  
**Applies to:** International, Intergovernmental, and Treaty-Level Contexts

---

## 1. Purpose

This declaration asserts DGCP™ readiness for alignment with international treaties, agreements, and cross-border governance frameworks.

---

## 2. Treaty Compatibility Principle

DGCP™ is designed to be:
- Jurisdiction-neutral
- Cryptographically verifiable
- Audit-compatible with international standards

---

## 3. Non-Assertion Clause

This declaration does not constitute:
- A treaty offer
- A legal agreement
- A binding international obligation

---

## 4. Future Alignment

Formal treaty participation requires:
- Explicit governance approval
- Separate legal instruments
- Public disclosure
