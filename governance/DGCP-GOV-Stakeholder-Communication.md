# DGCP Governance: Stakeholder Communication

**Effective Date:** 27 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Communications with DGCP™ Stakeholders

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines principles and standards for communicating with DGCP™ stakeholders.

---

## 2. Stakeholder Categories

Stakeholders may include:
- Contributors
- Auditors and reviewers
- Institutional partners
- Public observers

---

## 3. Communication Principles

All communications must be:
- Accurate and evidence-based
- Non-misleading
- Consistent with governance policies
- Respectful of contributor rights

---

## 4. Escalation & Clarification

Material misunderstandings require:
- Public clarification
- Evidence-backed correction
- Governance review where necessary
