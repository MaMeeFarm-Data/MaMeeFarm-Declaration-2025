# DGCP Governance: Public Transparency Standards

**Effective Date:** 26 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Public-Facing DGCP™ Disclosures

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines minimum transparency standards for DGCP™ public disclosures.

---

## 2. Transparency Commitments

DGCP™ commits to:
- Public governance documentation
- Verifiable proof references
- Clear disclosure of limitations
- Separation of narrative and authority

---

## 3. Disclosure Boundaries

Transparency does not require:
- Exposure of private raw data
- Disclosure of personal sensitive information
- Compromise of contributor safety

---

## 4. Governance Alignment

All disclosures must align with governance policies and licenses.
