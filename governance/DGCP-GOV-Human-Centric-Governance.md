# DGCP Governance: Human-Centric Governance Principle

**Effective Date:** 24 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Decisions Affecting Human Contributors

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy affirms that DGCP™ governance remains fundamentally human-centric despite reliance on cryptographic and automated systems.

---

## 2. Core Principle

Technology exists to serve human truth, not replace human judgment.

---

## 3. Human Safeguards

DGCP™ governance ensures:
- Respect for human dignity
- Context-aware decision-making
- Protection from algorithmic harm

---

## 4. Balance of Authority

Automation supports governance but never replaces accountable human oversight.
