# DGCP Governance: System Resilience & Continuity

**Effective Date:** 23 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Operational Scenarios

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance measures ensuring DGCP™ system resilience and continuity.

---

## 2. Continuity Principle

DGCP™ must remain verifiable and auditable despite:
- Platform failure
- Account suspension
- Network disruption
- Partial data loss

---

## 3. Resilience Mechanisms

Resilience is achieved through:
- Distributed evidence storage
- Public ledger replication
- Cryptographic timestamping
- Governance-driven recovery

---

## 4. Governance Authority

Continuity decisions are governed through transparent, public commits.
