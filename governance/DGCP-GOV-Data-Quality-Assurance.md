# DGCP Governance: Data Quality Assurance

**Effective Date:** 27 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Data, Proofs, and Registries

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines data quality assurance requirements to ensure DGCP™ data remains accurate, consistent, and fit for verification and audit.

---

## 2. Quality Dimensions

DGCP™ data quality is evaluated across:
- Accuracy (factual correctness)
- Completeness (required fields present)
- Consistency (no internal contradictions)
- Traceability (verifiable provenance)
- Timeliness (appropriate temporal context)

---

## 3. Quality Controls

Quality assurance relies on:
- Structured metadata standards
- Hash-based integrity checks
- Cross-reference validation (proof ↔ registry)
- Periodic quality review

---

## 4. Non-Destructive Correction

Quality issues are addressed via:
- Annotations
- Registry updates
- Versioned documentation

Historical data is never erased.
