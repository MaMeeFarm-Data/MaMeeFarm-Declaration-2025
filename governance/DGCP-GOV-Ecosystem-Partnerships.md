# DGCP Governance: Ecosystem Partnerships

**Effective Date:** 29 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Ecosystem Partnerships and Collaborations

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs partnerships within the DGCP™ ecosystem.

---

## 2. Partnership Principle

Partnerships must:
- Respect DGCP™ governance
- Preserve data ownership
- Maintain audit transparency

---

## 3. Partnership Boundaries

Partnerships do not grant:
- Data ownership
- Unrestricted access
- Governance influence

---

## 4. Approval & Oversight

All partnerships require:
- Explicit governance approval
- Defined scope and duration
- Ongoing oversight
