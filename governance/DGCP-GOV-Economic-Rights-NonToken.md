# DGCP Governance: Economic Rights (Non-Token Based)

**Effective Date:** 22 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Economic Utilization of DGCP™ Data

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines economic rights associated with DGCP™ data without relying on tokenization or speculative financial instruments.

---

## 2. Core Principle

Economic value under DGCP™ derives from:
- Verifiable real work
- Audit-ready data
- Governed licensing

DGCP™ does not require tokens, coins, or speculative assets to establish value.

---

## 3. Permitted Economic Activities

Permitted activities include:
- Licensing of verified datasets
- Research access agreements
- Institutional verification services
- Compliance and audit support services

---

## 4. Prohibited Economic Activities

Prohibited activities include:
- Token issuance backed by DGCP™ data
- Fractional ownership schemes
- Speculative derivatives referencing DGCP™ proofs

---

## 5. Enforcement

Violations result in immediate license revocation and public governance notice.
