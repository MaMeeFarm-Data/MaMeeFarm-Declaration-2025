# DGCP Governance: Safety-by-Design

**Effective Date:** 28 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ System Design and Implementation Decisions

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes safety-by-design as a core governance requirement within DGCP™.

---

## 2. Safety Principle

Safety considerations must be integrated at design time, not retrofitted after deployment.

---

## 3. Safety Dimensions

Safety includes:
- Contributor safety
- Data misuse prevention
- System misuse resistance
- Adversarial abuse mitigation

---

## 4. Governance Accountability

Safety design decisions must be:
- Documented
- Reviewable
- Aligned with risk and threat models
