# DGCP Governance: Emergency & Crisis Protocol

**Effective Date:** 25 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** System Emergencies and Crisis Scenarios

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance procedures during emergencies or crises affecting DGCPâ„¢ operations.

---

## 2. Crisis Scenarios

Scenarios include:
- Legal injunctions
- Platform-wide failures
- Coordinated adversarial attacks
- Data custody disruption

---

## 3. Emergency Authority

Temporary emergency actions may be taken to:
- Preserve evidence
- Secure audit trails
- Protect contributors

All actions must be documented and reviewed post-crisis.

---

## 4. Restoration

Normal governance resumes following documented resolution.
