# DGCP Governance: Federation & Interoperability

**Effective Date:** 29 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Federated DGCP™ Systems and Cross-System Interactions

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines governance rules for federation and interoperability between DGCP™ systems.

---

## 2. Federation Principle

Federation enables interoperability without:
- Merging governance authority
- Transferring ownership
- Diluting audit lineage

---

## 3. Interoperability Requirements

Federated systems must:
- Preserve original proof hashes
- Maintain independent governance
- Support cross-verification

---

## 4. Non-Inheritance Rule

Federated systems do not automatically inherit:
- Trust
- Certification
- Governance legitimacy
