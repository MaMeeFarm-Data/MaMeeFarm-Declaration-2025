# DGCP Governance: Privacy Preservation

**Effective Date:** 28 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Data Handling and Disclosure Activities

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines privacy preservation principles governing DGCP™ data across collection, storage, disclosure, and verification.

---

## 2. Core Privacy Principle

DGCP™ prioritizes verifiability without unnecessary exposure of personal or sensitive information.

Privacy preservation is a governance requirement, not an optional feature.

---

## 3. Privacy Controls

Controls include:
- Data minimization
- Context-aware disclosure
- Separation of raw private data from public proofs
- Governance-reviewed disclosure decisions

---

## 4. Disclosure Boundary

Public proofs must not expose:
- Personal identifiers
- Sensitive location data
- Information that may endanger contributors
