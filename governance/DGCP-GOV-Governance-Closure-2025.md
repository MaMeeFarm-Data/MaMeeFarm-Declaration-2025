# DGCP Governance: 2025 Closure Statement

**Effective Date:** 30 December 2025  
**Status:** Governance Closure Document  
**Scope:** End-of-Year Governance Consolidation

---

## Statement

This document formally closes the 2025 governance development cycle for MaMeeFarm™ DGCP™.

All governance domains required for:
- Ethical operation
- Institutional readiness
- Auditability
- Long-term resilience

have been defined, documented, and publicly committed.

---

## Forward Position

Future governance changes will:
- Build upon this foundation
- Be documented via changelog
- Preserve historical continuity

---

## Authority

This closure statement does not prevent governance evolution.  
It establishes a stable baseline for 2026 onward.
