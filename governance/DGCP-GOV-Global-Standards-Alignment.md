# DGCP Governance: Global Standards Alignment

**Effective Date:** 24 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Specifications and Implementations

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy declares DGCP™ alignment intent with recognized international standards bodies and frameworks.

---

## 2. Alignment Targets

DGCP™ is designed to be compatible with principles from:
- ISO (data governance, auditability)
- W3C (verifiable data models)
- OECD (data ethics and trust)
- UN data governance guidelines

---

## 3. Non-Certification Clause

This policy does not claim formal certification or endorsement by any standards body.

---

## 4. Evolution

Standards alignment is iterative and subject to governance review.
