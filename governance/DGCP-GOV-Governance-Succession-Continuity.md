# DGCP Governance: Succession & Continuity

**Effective Date:** 25 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** Governance Leadership and Authority Continuity

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy ensures continuity of DGCPâ„¢ governance in the event of leadership transition or incapacity.

---

## 2. Succession Principle

Governance authority must never depend on a single individual.

---

## 3. Continuity Mechanisms

Mechanisms include:
- Documented authority delegation
- Multi-signature governance approvals
- Public succession records

---

## 4. Transparency

All succession actions are:
- Publicly recorded
- Timestamped
- Auditable
