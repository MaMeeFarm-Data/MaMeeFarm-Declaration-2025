# DGCP Governance: International Audit & Certification Path

**Effective Date:** 28 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** External Audits and Certification Initiatives

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines a structured path for international audit and potential certification of DGCP™ governance and operations.

---

## 2. Audit Readiness

DGCP™ supports audits based on:
- Public governance documentation
- Cryptographic verification
- Transparent changelogs
- Evidence-backed reporting

---

## 3. Certification Boundary

This policy does not claim existing certification.

Any certification requires:
- Independent audit
- Formal engagement
- Public disclosure

---

## 4. Governance Control

All audit and certification engagements require explicit governance approval.
