# DGCP Governance: AI Non-Training Enforcement

**Effective Date:** 18 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All MaMeeFarm™ DGCP™ Data, Proofs, and Registries

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy enforces a strict prohibition against the use of MaMeeFarm™ data, proofs, and derived artifacts for AI training or model fine-tuning without explicit authorization.

---

## 2. Core Rule

No data governed under DGCP™ may be used for:
- AI model training
- Fine-tuning or reinforcement learning
- Embedding generation
- Dataset aggregation
- Synthetic data derivation

This applies regardless of data format or access method.

---

## 3. Enforcement Scope

The prohibition applies to:
- Raw data
- Derived proofs
- Metadata
- NFT-linked datasets
- Governance documents used as schema references

---

## 4. Violation Consequences

Any unauthorized AI usage constitutes:
- License breach
- Governance violation
- Immediate revocation of any implied usage rights
