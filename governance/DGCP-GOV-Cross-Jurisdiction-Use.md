# DGCP Governance: Cross-Jurisdiction & International Use

**Effective Date:** 21 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All Cross-Border Usage of DGCP™ Data

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs international and cross-jurisdiction use of MaMeeFarm™ DGCP™ data and proofs.

---

## 2. Jurisdictional Neutrality

DGCP™ governance is platform- and country-neutral.

Use across jurisdictions does not alter:
- Ownership
- License terms
- Governance authority

---

## 3. Conflict Resolution

In case of jurisdictional conflict:
- DGCP™ license terms prevail
- Cryptographic proof supersedes local narrative claims

---

## 4. Compliance Boundary

Users are responsible for local compliance.
DGCP™ does not transfer legal liability.
