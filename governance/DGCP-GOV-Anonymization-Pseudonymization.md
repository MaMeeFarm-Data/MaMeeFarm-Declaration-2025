# DGCP Governance: Anonymization & Pseudonymization

**Effective Date:** 28 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Data Transformations for Privacy Protection

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines acceptable anonymization and pseudonymization practices under DGCP™ governance.

---

## 2. Anonymization Principle

Anonymization must:
- Prevent re-identification
- Preserve audit relevance
- Maintain data integrity

---

## 3. Pseudonymization Use

Pseudonymization may be used when:
- Attribution is required
- Contributor protection is necessary
- Governance tracking must be preserved

---

## 4. Governance Review

All anonymization methods are subject to governance review to prevent false anonymity claims.
