# DGCP Governance: Data Lifecycle Management

**Effective Date:** 29 December 2025  
**Status:** Active Governance Policy  
**Applies to:** All DGCP™ Data, Proofs, and Registries

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the governed lifecycle of DGCP™ data from creation to archival.

---

## 2. Lifecycle Stages

DGCP™ data lifecycle includes:
- Creation (real-work data generation)
- Verification (hashing, timestamps, validation)
- Publication (public proofs and registries)
- Maintenance (annotations and governance updates)
- Archival (long-term preservation)

---

## 3. Lifecycle Integrity

At all stages:
- Provenance must remain traceable
- Integrity must be verifiable
- Governance rules must apply

---

## 4. Non-Destructive Principle

Lifecycle transitions must not erase historical truth.
